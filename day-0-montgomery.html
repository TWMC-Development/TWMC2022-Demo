Be a part of something bigger than yourself.

Welcome, neighbor! We are delighted to have you here at The Church at Woodforest. As a campus of The Woodlands Methodist Church, we value the importance of embracing visitors and making them feel welcome. Whether you are a long-time churchgoer or someone exploring faith, we believe everyone has a place in our church family.

We are here to help! Our church members or staff are here to assist with anything that might make your time with us more meaningful and fulfilling.

Once again, thank you for choosing to join us in worship. We hope that you find inspiration, peace and a sense of belonging here at The Church at Woodforest.

Frequently Asked Questions

Where do I start?

Welcome to The Church at Montgomery, a campus of The Woodlands Methodist Church! We are glad you are here. Please take time to explore our website to see all the worship services, Bible studies, classes, outreach activities and events we have to offer. Once you decide to visit, stop by Connection Point to meet our members and find out more information.

What style of worship do you offer?

Our Montgomery Campus offers modern liturgical worship. Services feature liturgical aspects of worship and classic hymns of the faith mixed with a modern service structure and current worship music.

Where do I park?

Our Montgomery Campus meets at Oak Hills Junior High, 19190 Keenan Cut Off Road. Parking is available on the east and north side of the building.

Do you offer child care on Sunday morning?

Nursery is available for ages 6 weeks through 2 years. When you arrive, you will see a check-in table for the nursery.

Is there programming for my children on Sunday morning?

We worship as a family, inviting all ages to join us. After a time of singing and a children’s message, we invite children, preschool through fifth grade, to attend Children’s Church. When you arrive to our building, you will see a sign-up table for Children’s Church.

On the first Sunday of the month, we worship as a family, so we can share Communion together.

Do you offer any resources for my child with special needs?

On our campus in The Woodlands, we have an active Special Needs Ministry for children, youth and adults. We offer worship, discipleship and social and recreational opportunities for those with special needs. We also strive to provide care and support for their family members and caregivers.

Do you offer adult Sunday school classes?

Our adult Connect Class meets Sunday mornings at 9:00 in the library at Oak Hills Junior High School, while several Home Groups meet throughout the week and throughout the community. To find out how to get connected, email Pastor David Lindwall.

Is there student ministry on Sunday morning?

Our student ministry serves students in grades 6 – 12. Our Sunday morning services are geared for family worship; however, our junior high and high school students have their own additional meeting time. Junior high and high school students are currently meeting on Wednesday nights for fellowship, games and interactive Bible study with students at The Church at Woodforest, another campus of The Woodlands Methodist Church. Gatherings specifically for students at The Church at Montgomery will be added soon.

How long is a normal service?

Services last about an hour.

Where can I find more information about how TWMC serves the community?

Our church mission statement is Reach People for Jesus Christ, Disciple Them in Faith, and Help Those in Need. That last part — Help Those in Need — is the heartbeat of our church. It is interwoven in and throughout all our ministries and driven by our Missions Ministry. Because we love Jesus Christ, we have hearts of compassion to help those in need.

We have many local and global partners who help us faithfully serve God’s people. Our local partners in Montgomery County, the Houston area and Texas provide opportunities to serve our neighbors . Our global partners work with us to provide mission trip opportunities for us to be the hands and feet of Jesus around the world.

How do I let someone know I have a prayer request?

Prayer is a powerful thing, and we have a committed group of clergy and trusted prayer leaders that will lift up your prayer request throughout the week. Call Caring Ministries at 281.297.5900 or request prayer online.
