THE WOODLANDS CAMPUS

We receive new members every weekend after each of our on-campus worship services in the Chapel, the Sanctuary and the Harvest Worship Center. When you exit the worship service, look for a banner that says “Join Here.” We have volunteers who will be there and will walk you through the new member process.

If you are worshipping at the Loft, go to the Connection Point and a volunteer will meet you to guide you through the new member process.

Join Here

Through Join Here, you will learn about membership, our ministries, our worship communities and how you can become involved. If you are ready, join the church with other visitors and make The Woodlands Methodist Church your new church home.

MONTGOMERY CAMPUS

If you wish to join, click the “Desire to Join” button above, and Pastor Aaron Laird will reach out to you with the next steps. On the last Sunday of each month, you will be asked to stand along with all other members — you will not be singled out or asked to come to the front. As a group, you will affirm your vows of membership and be personally welcomed into The Church at Montgomery family.
