RADIANT BIBLE STUDY

Designed to ignite your spirit and deepen your understanding of the Word, this transformative experience is more than just a study — it’s a radiant gathering of women seeking growth, knowledge and profound connections. Discover timeless truths of Scripture while nurturing meaningful relationships with fellow sisters in faith. Through weekly teaching, insightful discussion and guided study of Scripture, you’ll uncover the beauty of God’s Word and its relevance to your life today. Together, we’ll grow in biblical knowledge and cultivate authentic relationships that reflect the love of Jesus.

What to expect?

Radiant Bible Study

Radiant Bible Study for Women has both a spring and fall study within the calendar year. In all sessions, Radiant is based on large group teaching either live or via video. Small group time follows the large group teaching. Radiant does include a workbook, and homework varies from semester to semester depending on the study.

The average small group size is 15, although some groups may be larger and some smaller. Returning participants may choose to remain with their group each semester or choose to join a new group. We do our best to honor friend requests; however, the size of the group is a determining factor as well. You can expect the small group time to include discussion from the workbook and large group teaching, relationship building and prayer.

Each semester Radiant Bible Study for Women participates in an outreach service project such as collecting items for the Women’s Medical Mission Trip, Single Moms group and Welcome Refugees.

Through our registration, you will be able to let us know if you prefer to meet in person or participate in the study online using Zoom (evening session only, open to all campuses).

FALL RADIANT

This fall, step into the stories of remarkable women from the Old Testament with Radiant Bible Study in “Stories of Redemption: Women of the Old Testament.” These women’s lives, though ordinary, played extraordinary roles in God’s redemptive plan. Through engaging lessons, led by Rev. Luann Riley, discover how their legacy of faith still speaks to us today. Registration required.
