Children spend the weeks leading up to Christmas in a state of excitement, anticipating all the joys Christmas Day will bring. However, in the hustle and bustle of our fast-paced lives, it has become all too easy to overlook the wonder that should fill this special time. We speed through the season rather than anticipate and celebrate it. In the waiting, the hopeful expectation of what is to come, we uncover the true miracle of the season — the realization of the promise of love and joy captured in Jesus’ birth.

Anticipation is not only a countdown to a day on the calendar, but a journey of the heart. This holiday season, we invite you to join us at The Woodlands Methodist Church as we pause, take a deep breath and rediscover the awe of this holy season. Bring your family and friends as we embrace the divine wonder of Christmas.
