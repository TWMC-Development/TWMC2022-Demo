MEN'S NIGHT DETAILS

Monday, April 29, 2024
6:30 p.m.
The Woodlands Campus | The Loft Building
$25.00 per person

Purchase Tickets

We are calling all men to go beyond where you are in your faith and live a life fully devoted to God. If you’re a man who’s wondering what to do with your strength, your longings and your gifts, join Rev. Pierce Drake for an evening with Jon Tyson and Jefferson Bethke, authors of Fighting Shadows: Overcoming 7 Lies That Keep Men From Becoming Fully Alive.

You’ll receive an incredible message from Jon Tyson as he brings this challenge along with practical teaching and encouragement. Also, experience powerful worship led by Rev. Mark Swayze and Bristol House. You do not want to miss this exciting evening.

Special Guests

Jon Tyson – Speaker

Senior Pastor of Church of the City New York, Author of Fighting Shadows: Overcoming 7 Lies That Keep Men From Becoming Fully Alive

Learn more

Jon Tyson

Jon Tyson is a New York City based pastor and author. Originally from Adelaide, Australia, Jon moved to the United States 20 years ago with a passion to seek and cultivate renewal in the Western Church. He is the author of Rumors of God, Sacred Roots, A Creative Minority, The Burden Is Light, Beautiful Resistance, and The Intentional Father. He serves as the Lead Pastor of Church of the City New York

Jefferson Bethke

Author of Fighting Shadows: Overcoming 7 Lies That Keep Men From Becoming Fully Alive

Learn more

Jefferson Bethke

Jefferson Bethke is the New York Times bestselling author of Jesus > Religion and It's Not What You Think. He and his wife, Alyssa, host The Real Life Podcast and run FamilyTeams.com, an online initiative equipping families to live as a multi-generational team on a mission. They live in Maui with their daughters, Kinsley and Lucy, and son, Kannon. To say hi or to learn more, go to jeffandalyssa.com
