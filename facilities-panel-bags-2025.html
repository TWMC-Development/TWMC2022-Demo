Oversized Bag Policy

Policy Overview

In an effort to enhance the safety and protection of our members and guests, we have established a strict policy prohibiting backpacks and similar-sized bags in worship services. Any bag in question will be subject to immediate search by a member of the Montgomery County Sheriff's Office (MCSO) or our security team. This policy is intended to keep our congregation and visitors safe and free from distractions during our worship services.

When

During all worship and special services (i.e. Sunday Morning, Funerals/Memorials, Mid-week Prayer). This policy applies only to worship and special services. Conferences or other events may have their own security protocols.

Examples of types of bags not allowed in worship venues

  • Backpacks
  • Bookbags
  • Briefcases
  • Sling Bags
  • Duffle Bags
  • Rolling Luggage
  • Computer Bags

Exceptions

  • Parents with diaper bags (must have child present)
  • Individuals with medical needs (e.g., equipment, medication, and oxygen)
  • Volunteers or staff transporting approved ministry materials
  • Video team with camera bags
  • Tech team with production cases or gear

Those who bring one of the restricted bags to a venue service will kindly be asked to return it to their vehicle or store it for safe keeping in a designated area.

Thank you for helping to keep us all safe!
