Single Life Ministry Painted Churches Tour

All Singles

Day trip

Monday, October 6, 2025
Depart TWMC Aldersgate Entrance (Entrance 4) at 8:15 a.m.
618 N. Main St., Schulenburg, Texas →
No child care provided
$60.00 per person (covers transportation to/from Schulenburg and tour). Each person is responsible for their own lunch and $10 gratuity for tour guide.

Join us for a tour of the world-famous painted churches located in the communities of Dubina, High Hill, Praha and Ammannsville. They appear unassuming as you approach them, but as you enter the front doors, prepare to have your breath taken away by the incredible beauty found inside. We will have our own personal tour guide who will join us the entire day and share a wealth of knowledge about each church’s history.
