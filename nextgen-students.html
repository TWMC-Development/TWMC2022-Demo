NextGen Students

“LOVE JESUS AND HAVE FUN DOING IT.”
– BOB SWAN

Welcome to Rebelbase Student Ministry! Students from 7th to 12th grade move beyond the basics – we want to help students answer the question, "What's next?" We want them to grow in their faith, take ownership of their relationships with Jesus and honor God with their lives. Everyone is welcome. We can't wait to meet you!

Crowded House

High School Small Groups

High School Small Groups are the heartbeat behind our ministry. Our hope is that each High School student is part of a small group, intentionally being poured into by an adult leader. Each group is divided by age and gender and will meet weekly to dig into the Word and build a community that supports each other through all of High School's ups and downs.

NextGen Students With Special Needs

REMIX – Young Adults

REMIX serves teens with special needs from 7th grade through high school graduation. We offer Sunday morning programs as well as other social and recreational opportunities. Our desire is to share the love of Christ with your student and help them build Godly relationships that will last a lifetime.

We offer Sunday School at 9:30 and 11:00 a.m. in the Loft Building, Room LN103. Students can mainstream into the 11:00 a.m. Rebelbase Youth program or attend one of our sensory-friendly classes at either 9:30 or 11:00 a.m with developmentally appropriate Bible lessons. Special Needs Ministry Volunteers and Interns are assigned to assist students in either program.

Find out more about our Special Needs Ministry.
