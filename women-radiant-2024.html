RADIANT BIBLE STUDY

Too often, we find ourselves worn out by the cares of everyday life, but we were never intended to rely on our own strength. We have been given the love, strength, and joy of Jesus shines through us and makes us RADIANT!

Radiant Bible Study has both a spring and fall study within the calendar year. In all of our sessions, Radiant is based on large group teaching either live or via video. Small group time follows the large group teaching. Radiant Bible Study does include a workbook and homework varies from semester-to-semester depending on the study.

The average small group size is fifteen, although some groups may be larger, and some may be smaller. Returning participants may choose to remain with their group each semester or choose to join a new group. We do our best to honor friend request; however, the size of the group is a determining factor as well. You can expect the small group time to include discussion from the workbook, large group teaching, relationship building, and prayer.

Each semester Radiant Bible Study participates in an outreach service project such as collecting items for Women's Medical Mission Trip, Single Moms, or Welcome Refugees.

Through our registration, you will be able to let us know if you prefer to meet in person or do the study online using Zoom (evening session only open to all campuses).
