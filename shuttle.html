What to know

The holidays can be a busy time, so we've made parking as seamless as possible for our Christmas Eve Services. Here is everything you need to know to make your visit stress-free.

  • When? Shuttles are available for 3:00 PM to 9:00 PM Christmas Eve services on The Woodlands Campus.
  • Where? Off-site parking at Whole Foods parking garage , levels 3 & 4, with shuttle drop-off at Atrium Entrance 1 .

Give the Gift of Parking this Christmas Eve!

By choosing off-site parking you're helping to make room for new visitors on Christmas Eve. Together, we can share a simple act of love and compassion with others during such a special time of year.
