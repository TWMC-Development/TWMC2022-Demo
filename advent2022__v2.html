JOIN OUR CHRISTMAS SERVICES

Check our schedule

Sometimes Christmas falls short of the ideal experience we imagine it should be. We give up hope that we will have the picture-perfect holiday. Then we remember that first Christmas with no room in the inn, a manger for a bed and animals for the guests. In the midst of that imperfection, Jesus Christ, our perfect hope, was born.

This holiday season, we invite you to join us at The Woodlands Methodist Church as we discover It’s (Not Always) a Wonderful Life: Real Life Meets Real Hope. Bring your friends and family and celebrate a perfectly imperfect Christmas with us.

Emotional Support

Counseling

The holidays bring out different emotions in each of us. Sometimes it can be a time when we struggle to feel festive like those around us. We want to walk beside you and assure you of God’s presence as you navigate the holiday season.

Prayer Ministry

If you’re experiencing some difficult days during this holiday season because you’ve lost a loved one, or life is just hard right now, we’re here to support you. We offer pastoral counseling, support groups and prayer teams to help you find the hope of Christ.
