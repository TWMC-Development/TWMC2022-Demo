Announcing Vision Night

We have set aside the evening of Sunday, May 7, 2023, for Vision Night and voting for future affiliation. Our special program begins at 6:00 p.m. in the main sanctuary and will conclude with member-only voting beginning at 7:00 p.m.

Remote voting will be offered online, in addition to in-person voting in the church office. Voting will be open from 7:00 p.m. Sunday, May 7, through 4:30 p.m. Thursday, May 11. Preregistration is NOT required. Results of our congregational affiliation vote will be announced in all worship services on Mother's Day weekend, May 13-14, 2023.
