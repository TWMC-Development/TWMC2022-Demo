Come Up Higher

Prayer Conference 2024

Speaker Rev. Jordan Wilson | Bristol House Worship

Do you long to grow in your prayer life with Jesus? Join us for “Come Up Higher,” Prayer Conference 2024. God invites us to rise above the noise of the world and experience a greater intimacy with Christ.

Hear from Rev. Jordan Wilson, who strives to equip the church with greater authority to pray God’s Word and to hear Him as He speaks to us. Worship with Bristol House Music and spend time in fellowship and prayer.

Rev. Jordan Wilson

Jordan Wilson serves as the Associate Pastor at Stillwater Church in Dayton, Ohio, where she is helping to raise up a family of disciples across generations. Her passion is to equip the church in knowing how to cooperate with the Holy Spirit through worship, prayer and inner healing ministry. She longs to see the Bride of Christ fully alive so that Jesus receives His full reward. Jordan received her Master of Divinity with an emphasis on Holy Spirit and Church Renewal at United Theological Seminary, and she is an ordained Elder in the Global Methodist Church.
