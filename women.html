Find Community With Other Women

WOMEN MADE FOR CONNECTION

We believe one of the beautiful aspects of being created in God’s image is being made for connection, for community. Having a place where we can share what’s on our hearts and minds, make friends, and learn from one another is crucial for us as we grow into the Christ-like women God intended for us to be.

We have many opportunities for you to fit in at The Woodlands Methodist Church. Through our book clubs, Bible studies, small groups and social events, you’ll find community with other women while growing closer with God.
