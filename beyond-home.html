BEYOND Capital Campaign Update

 

A MESSAGE FROM OUR SENIOR PASTOR

Dear friends,

Over the past few years, we’ve witnessed God’s love grow in our community, and now we’re invited to step into something greater. Today, I want to introduce you to Beyond — a capital campaign that calls us to go further in faith, generosity and impact. Beyond is more than a goal; it’s a shared vision to expand our ministry, deepen our service and reach hearts in ways we’ve never imagined. It’s a call to create a lasting impact and expand our reach for the kingdom of God. The work God is calling us to in this next season will require all of us — our faith, our resources and, most importantly, our trust that God is at work in ways we cannot yet fully see. This is not a journey we can walk alone. This is our moment as a church family to trust God’s provision and be part of something that will bless lives for years to come. As you read through this campaign summary, I want to encourage you to prayerfully consider how God is inviting you to be a part of this mission. How is God calling you to go beyond?

Rev. Mark Sorensen
Senior Pastor

CALLED TO GO FURTHER

"Called to Go Further" is a powerful and evocative tagline that encapsulates our sense of purpose, action, and continuous growth. It aligns perfectly with our "Beyond" theme, emphasizing a divine and collective call to extend our efforts and push boundaries. This phrase subtly resonates with our mission statement—"Reach People for Jesus, Disciple Them in Faith, and Help Those in Need"—suggesting a dedication to outreach, nurturing faith, and service without being overly explicit. It motivates and inspires our community to embrace their divine calling, take decisive action, and continuously seek growth and greater impact.

COMMITTED TO REACHING PEOPLE

The Woodlands Methodist Church is committed to reaching people for Jesus, discipling them in faith and helping those in need. Through the Beyond campaign, we’re expanding our impact with three key initiatives:

  1. Montgomery County Food Bank – Funding facility expansion to serve 5,000 more families monthly and provide 1 million additional meals.
  2. Church Planting in Latin America – Equipping pastors to launch vibrant churches and grow faith-filled communities.
  3. Zoe Empowers Eswatini – Expanding efforts to help vulnerable youth gain skills, stability and hope. With a $1 million commitment, we are answering God’s call to go beyond — feeding families, planting churches and empowering youth. Your support makes this mission possible!
