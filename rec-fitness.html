MOVING IN FAITH

No Cost

This workout is movement to music, drawing from the traditions of martial arts and dance. For everybody, ages 12-100, and all fitness levels. This workout is done barefoot (shoes if needed) and conditions mind, body, heart and spirit.

This practice includes a monthly scripture and recovery focus, warmup, get moving, cool down, relaxation and prayer.

This community of women who choose movement is inspiring, and encouraging.

Moving your body can create momentum in life...come dance with us.

Women community
Goals: flexibility, agility, mobility, strength, and stability
Tuesdays | 5:45 – 6:45 p.m. | Fitness Room 2
Wednesdays | 11:30 a.m. – 12:45 p.m. | Fitness Room 1

WOODLANDS TAI CHI

No Cost

The Woodlands Tai Chi is a completely voluntary community of seniors and residents of Montgomery County. It is open to those who are interested in the exercise of mental and physical health. The goal is to offer seniors and residents of Montgomery County a productive and safe activity to help improve their bodies and minds.

Seniors and residents of Montgomery County community
Wednesdays | 8:00 – 11:00 a.m. | Rec. Activity Room 1
Fridays | 8:00 – 11:00 a.m. | Rec. Activity Room 1
The Woodlands Tai Chi classes are free, purchase of WTC shirt is required for all new beginners class.
