Formation and discipleship in the church are all about the process of living out and learning about the teachings of Christ. This process is about more than just head knowledge; it’s about reorienting our entire lives around His teachings and way of life. In this process, we learn to live in the reality of the kingdom of God here and now. Through spiritual disciplines like prayer, Scripture reading and community, we are formed into the image of Christ. It’s a journey of transformation, where we don’t just learn about Jesus but learn to live like Jesus, cultivating a life marked by love, joy, peace and deep, authentic relationship with God and others.

Formation

Formation is not merely about acquiring knowledge — it’s about transforming one’s life to reflect the love, grace and truth of Jesus. Faith should not only be understood but also actively practiced, fostering a vibrant and faithful community. This is the heart of what it means to follow Jesus and be His disciples in the world today.

Groups are the center for connection and formation at The Woodlands Methodist Church. Through home groups, we strive to encounter the tangible presence of God, be transformed into Christ’s image, and live in the way of Jesus.

John Wesley, the founder of Methodism, created pathways and rhythms for walking in joy, freedom and holiness in Christ. One such pathway was “banding,” when a group of three to five same-gendered people would meet, read and pray together. Learn more about banding together at TWMC as we seek to encourage spiritual accountability and growth.

Spiritual mentorship at The Woodlands Methodist Church provides guidance, wisdom and support, helping individuals become more like Jesus, one step at a time. The mentoring process fosters personal growth, accountability and a deeper understanding of one’s faith.

Discipleship

Being a disciple involves ongoing learning, personal growth and helping others. Adult Discipleship offers studies and classes, with various days and times, to dive deeper into the Bible in community. Whether you’re new to the faith or have been studying the Bible for years, all are welcome as we explore, ask questions and encourage one another to deepen our relationship with God through His Word.

Trying to be more like Jesus and becoming the people we’re intended to be is a lot better in community. Whether you’re exploring faith as a newcomer or seeking deeper growth in a long-standing journey, The Woodlands Methodist Church offers a variety of studies, events and groups tailored to support you.

Studies and Classes

Our singles ministry at The Woodlands Methodist Church is an ideal environment to deepen your faith and enjoy meaningful connections with other single adults. It’s a place where individuals in similar life stages can strengthen their relationship with God, find support and celebrate the unique journey of single life together.

Singles

There’s a unique understanding among men — we connect in a way that’s deeply rooted in our shared experiences and challenges. At The Woodlands Methodist Church, we recognize this camaraderie. We offer spaces where you can engage openly and authentically with other men, discussing issues relevant to your journey toward becoming a man of Christ-like character.

Men

At The Woodlands Methodist Church, we cherish the truth that as women created in God’s image, we are designed for deep connection and community. It’s in these spaces where we can openly share our hearts, build meaningful friendships and glean wisdom from each other — a crucial part of our journey toward becoming the Christ-like women God intended us to be.
