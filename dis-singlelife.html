Angel Reach – Angel’s Loft Resale Shop

Foster Care Ministry

Family-Friendly Event

Saturday, April 6, 2024
9:00 a.m. – noon | 8:45 a.m. — arrive at Angel’s Loft Resale Shop
6 volunteers needed
Off-site | 2121 Timber Lane, Conroe, TX 77301
Come prepared with closed toe shoes
Child care is not provided for this off-campus activity

Project: sort donations, size the racks, dust furnishings, and general shop organization and clean up

Angel Reach serves foster/kinship families and youth who have aged out of the foster care system, as well as homeless and at-risk youth. Angel's Loft sales support our organization’s ongoing mission to help kinship families, aged out foster youth and those at-risk of homelessness in the community.

Learn more at angelreach.org/angels-loft

Compassion United Food Pantry — Terry’s Table

At-Risk Families

Family-Friendly Event

Saturday, April 6, 2024
8:30 a.m. – 12:30 p.m. | 8:15 a.m. — arrive at Terry’s Table
16 volunteers needed
Children ages 10 years and older may participate with adult supervision
Off-site | 18856 FM 1484 Road, Conroe, TX 77303
Wear closed toe shoes, bring work gloves and water
Child care is not provided for this off-campus activity

Project: package, sort and deliver food to those in need at Terry’s Table in Cut-N-Shoot, the poorest part of Montgomery County.

Volunteers will

  • Provide a friendly welcome
  • Help sort and package, and/or help take the groceries out to the families' vehicles.
  • Pray with families

Compassion United (Homeless Ministry) partners with the Montgomery County Food Bank’s (MCFB) Food Distributions to provide nutritious food, including fresh produce and refrigerated items, directly to communities with a high need.

Learn more at mcfoodbank.org or compassionunited.us
