Membership

BECOMING A MEMBER

We would love for you to become a part of The Woodlands Methodist Church family. Our church community promises to nurture your faith and walk with you through the journeys of life. As you get involved in small groups, Bible studies or missions in our worship communities and on our campuses, you will enjoy faith-filled relationships with others.

If you are interested in joining the church, click the button below and a staff member will get in touch with you.

THE WOODLANDS CAMPUS

We receive new members every weekend after each of our on-campus worship services in the Chapel, the Sanctuary and the Harvest Worship Center. When you exit the worship service, look for a banner that says “Join Here.” We have volunteers who will be there and will walk you through the new member process.

If you are worshipping at the Loft, go to the Connection Point and a volunteer will meet you to guide you through the new member process.

Join Here

Through Join Here, you will learn about membership, our ministries, our worship communities and how you can become involved. If you are ready, join the church with other visitors and make The Woodlands Methodist Church your new church home.

WOODFOREST CAMPUS

We receive new members at bi-monthly Back Porch events. Upon completion of your membership vows, we will jump-start your journey into serving, giving, prayer and fellowship. Lunch and child care will be provided.

MONTGOMERY CAMPUS

If you wish to join, click the “Desire to Join” button above, and Pastor Aaron Laird will reach out to you with the next steps. On the last Sunday of each month, you will be asked to stand along with all other members — you will not be singled out or asked to come to the front. As a group, you will affirm your vows of membership and be personally welcomed into The Church at Montgomery family.

CREEKSIDE CAMPUS

Pizza with the Pastors

At Covenant Church at Creekside, we believe relationships are at the heart of our faith journeys. That’s why our membership process starts with Pizza with the Pastors. This informal lunch provides an opportunity to get to know one another. We’d love to talk with you, discuss what it means to be a member of Covenant and answer any questions that you may have about the church or the membership process.

Schedule

Sundays | 11:45 a.m. – 1:00 p.m. | Crust Pizza Creekside

When you’ve decided to join the church, click the “Desire to Join” button above and a staff member will get in touch with you.
