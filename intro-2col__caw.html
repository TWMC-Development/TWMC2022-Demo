What to Expect

The Church at Woodforest is a campus of The Woodlands Methodist Church. Worship gatherings are held in the Worship Center at 700 Fish Creek Thoroughfare. Worship services are filled with live worship music and engaging Bible teaching. Families and individuals of all ages are invited to join this “front porch” community of believers.
