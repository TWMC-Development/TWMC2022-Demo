The Woodlands Methodist Church Young Adults Ministry exists to lead young adults, ages 18-29, as they transition into the life of the church as self-giving, mature adults actively participating in the mission of God. We also seek to integrate into the life and rhythms of The Woodlands Methodist Church family.
