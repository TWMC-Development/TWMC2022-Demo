The discernment leadership team has developed a list of six fundamental and distinctive criteria — what we call discernment frameworks — that have served as a measurement tool for comparison of affiliation options: Mission, Theology, Operations, Culture, Community and Vision. Learn more about these categories at twmc.org/affiliation-framework

Currently TWMC is considering two affiliation options:

  • Global Methodist Church
