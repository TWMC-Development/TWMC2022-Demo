WAYS TO GIVE

In addition to donating in person during our weekend worship services, you can also give in the following ways:

Online Giving

Make a payment using our secure, online payment form. Gifts can be made by credit card or bank account and can also be set up to automatically recur at a frequency of your choosing.

Donate Now Recurring Gift

By Check

Write a check payable to The Woodlands Methodist Church and mail to:
2200 Lake Woodlands Drive
The Woodlands, Texas 77380

Text to Give

Text the number 281.500.8400 and enter a dollar amount in the message field. All gifts will go to the General Fund unless otherwise specified. If you would like to preference your gift, you may use one of the following keywords: HARVEST, LOFT, ESPANOL, WOODFOREST, MONTGOMERY, MISSIONS or SOSS followed by a space and dollar amount. Example: HARVEST 50

Honorariums and Memorials

Make a one-time donation in honor or memory of a loved one.

Give Stock or Mutual Funds

Contact the {[ emailform email:'finance@twmc.org' contactname:'Finance Office' subject:'New Stock or Mutual Fund Inquiry' ]} for brokerage account information.

Donate a Vehicle

Contact {[ emailform email:'jrobson@twmc.org' contactname:'Jonah Robson' subject:'New Vehicle Donation Inquiry' ]} for more information.

Leave a Legacy

Give to The Woodlands Methodist Church Foundation. To discuss possible estate gifts and/or bequests, please contact {[ emailform email:'finance@twmc.org' contactname:'the Finance Office' subject:'New Leave a Legacy Inquiry' ]}.
