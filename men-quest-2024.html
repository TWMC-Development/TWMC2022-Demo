QUEST

For more than a decade, Quest Men’s Fellowship has been a place for men of all ages to grow in their faith and to become the kind of men God wants them to be.

For eight weeks in the fall and eight weeks in the spring, Rev. Pierce Drake and team will teach you what it takes to be a Godly man through examples from the Bible, history and pop culture.

SPRING 2024

ARE WE THERE YET? STORIES FROM THE WILDERNESS

We know that no matter where we find ourselves in the journey, we find the unknown before us. Join us as we look at the story of Moses and the life, lessons and stories along his journey. Sunday Nights start back on January 21 at 7:00 p.m. and Tuesday mornings on January 23 at 6:00 a.m.
