Welcome to FORGE

Forge is a conference style student awakening experience. Our goal is to encourage awakening and revival among students through worship, fellowship, learning from leaders within the awakening movement, small group discussion and service. Our prayer is the Holy Spirit will ignite a fire in and equip your students to evangelize others in their community.

Events will take place Friday evening February 17th through Sunday morning February 19th. We will worship, have small group discussion, learning from relevant teachers within the awakening movement, and have FUN. Service is also a major component to our weekend.

Look for more details around programming and service opportunity closer to the event. If you are joining us from another city or state we recommend researching VRBO for the most cost effective accommodation for your group. It is our experience that VRBO is the least expensive option for lodging in The Woodlands. There are several hotels near our campus and we are happy to reach out and get a discounted rate for your group.
