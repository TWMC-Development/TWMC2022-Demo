Welcome!

Welcome to The Woodlands Methodist Church! We’re so glad you found us! Like most families, life is probably busy and a little chaotic these days. Our church is here to help you navigate the many challenges facing individuals and families. Welcome to Church Online

We’re glad you stopped by for a visit. We hope you enjoy a more interactive online church experience with Church Online.

The new platform allows you to actually interact with others through joining the live chat features or sharing your live prayer requests. It’s a more participatory experience where you can take advantage of note taking and sharing, along with helpful Bible app integration. You can access Church Online from anywhere – a tablet, computer or mobile device. While being online won’t replace being in a live worship service, we think you will find this to be a viable complement to experiencing church in person.

DEDICATED TO SUPPORTING

We are dedicated to supporting people with special needs and their families through ministry that brings the love of Jesus into their lives. We also strive to provide care and support for their family members. We offer worship, discipleship, and social and recreational opportunities for people of all ages with special needs.

Volunteers with our Special Needs Ministry are paired with our special needs members in our Special Blessings, Youth and Adults ministries to assist them on Sunday mornings and show them the love of Christ.
