Meeting Format

Each age group meets in a large group for worship and the main lesson and then they break up into small groups for age-appropriate discussion and activities.

Children with Special Needs

We have Sunday School classes for children with special needs in preschool through 6th grade on the Main Campus and in the Loft.

Nursery (6 weeks - 12 years)

Children are precious to us at The Woodlands Methodist Church. We strive to make our Noah’s Nook Nursery fun and stimulating for even the youngest child coming through our door. While we realize it can be difficult to leave your child in the care of others, we want to assure you that our staff has a genuine love for the work they do.

Our Nursery is staffed by paid, experienced workers who are trained in the areas of child care, child development, safety and sanitation and certified by the American Heart Association in CPR and First Aid.

We offer a loving, Christian atmosphere. All of our rooms are safe, fun and filled with age-appropriate toys, games and books.

Early Childhood (3, Pre-K, and Kindergarten)

Our main program includes dozens of weekly volunteers to help support a small group of children. A core Early Childhood staff and interns help to organize, prepare and set up lessons and activities each week. Our young children participate in large group praise and worship where they sing songs and hear about Jesus. Volunteers oversee small groups in age specific classrooms in order to focus on the biblical truths that our children are learning. Children in our Sunday school program must be 3 years old by September 1 of the current school year.

Elementary (1st - 4th grade)

Our 1st – 4th grade Sunday School program is designed to encourage and nurture the spiritual understanding and growth of our elementary-aged children. Children are welcomed by the friendly faces of staff ministry leaders, adult volunteers and teenage interns to join us in building friendships, enjoying fellowship, singing praise and discovering biblical truths. Each year, our 3rd graders participate in Bible Sunday where they receive their own Bible during the Traditional worship service.

Creekside Campus

Sunday School

NextGen Kids at Covenant Church at Creekside is a safe place for kids to learn, explore and discover who they are in Christ. Children are important members of our church family. We want them to be part of our community, make friends of all ages and learn about their faith. In NextGen Kids Sunday school, your kids will worship, learn and have fun at their own level.

Sunday School Times

Beginning March 30

9:00 and 11:00 a.m.

Meeting Locations

  • 3 – 5 years old: Room 4
  • Kindergarten – 5th Grade: The Cove

Check In

Check-in area is located just outside the main entrance. Child care workers will meet your family, check your child into the system and walk you through how NextGen Kids works.

3 – 5 Years Old

  • Parents check in their young children to receive a printed name tag and a security tag.
  • Parents place name tag on child’s back, keep security tag for pick up and escort child to classroom.
  • Upon pick up, parents will show security tag to the child care worker.

Kindergarten – 5th Grade

  • Older children start in worship with their families and will be dismissed to NextGen Kids during the worship service.
  • Adult helpers will walk them to The Cove for assembly and class time.
  • Children will return to their families for the conclusion of the worship service.

WHAT TO EXPECT

3 – 5 Years Old
Our staff with 3 – 5 year olds are background checked and Safe Sanctuary trained. They help little ones learn about God’s Word at their own level. The stories read each week correspond to those learned by the older children and are accompanied by fun crafts and activities.

Kindergarten – 5th Grade
We believe it’s important for kids to gradually learn to participate in “big” worship. During the sermon time, elementary kids will be dismissed and escorted to The Cove for a special time of fun, discovery and learning. There they will gather with other students their age so they can learn about God’s Word at their own pace and level.

Nursery (6 weeks – 2 Years Old)

On Sundays, loving nursery care is available throughout the entire service for infants and toddlers. Our nursery staff are consistent, experienced, Safe Sanctuary trained and background checked, so you can be sure that your little ones are in excellent hands.

Children are precious to us at Covenant Church at Creekside. We strive to make our nursery fun and stimulating for even the youngest child coming through our door. While we realize it can be difficult to leave your child in the care of others, we want to assure you that our staff has a genuine love for the work they do.

We offer a loving, Christian atmosphere. Our rooms are safe, fun and filled with age-appropriate toys, games and books.

Child Care Reservations

Reservations are not necessary for worship services. Nursery care is provided at no cost for worship services.
