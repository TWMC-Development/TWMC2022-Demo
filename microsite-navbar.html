Denominational Affiliation | 2022

Forget the former things; do not dwell on the past. See, I am doing a new thing! Now it springs up; do you not perceive it? I am making a way in the wilderness and streams in the wasteland. — Isaiah 43:18-19

Following a successful vote to disaffiliate from the greater United Methodist denomination, the time has come for our congregation to discern the best options for future affiliation — whether it’s to join a different Methodist denomination or remain an independent church but affiliated with a network of other large Methodist congregations.

Standing at the crossroads of change, the TWMC community is reflecting on 5 years of ministry rooted in traditional, Wesleyan Christianity. Even greater, this community is seeking God’s leading and provision to boldly advance in a new era of ministry.

There are many details to consider as the church moves forward in faith. Join us by following the steps below.

Discernment Pathway

Pray

Discernment begins with listening for the voice of the Holy Spirit. We are developing a new resource for community prayer.
