FAQ

What is the date/time of the event?

Thursday, December 5, 2024
Check-in opens at 5:30 p.m.
Dessert begins at 6:00 p.m.
Sanctuary doors open at 7:00 p.m.
The speaker event begins at 7:30 p.m.
The event will conclude around 9:00 p.m.

Where do I check in?

You do not need to check in. If you need help on the night of the event, an information desk will be available in the Atrium.

Do I need to bring a ticket?

You do not need to bring a ticket for the dessert or speaker portions of the evening.

How do I find what room I’m in or my table number for dessert?

You will receive an email toward the end of November with your room and table assignment. If you need help on the night of the event, an information desk will be available in the Atrium.

Where do I park?

Parking lots B, C, D, E, F, and G on the east side of the church are the closest to the Sanctuary where Jennifer Rothschild will be speaking. Follow the signage posted in the parking lots.

Where do I go if I have a Dessert and Worship ticket?

Dessert takes place in various locations throughout TWMC, specifically Wesley Hall, Hayes Hall A and B, Asbury Hall and Aldersgate Hall. You will receive an email toward the end of November with your room and table number assignment. If you don’t remember your room or table number assignment, an information desk will be available in the Atrium on the evening of December 5.

Where do I go if I have a Speaker Only ticket?

Jennifer Rothschild will be speaking in the Sanctuary. Doors open at 7:00 p.m., and the event begins at 7:30 p.m.

Where do I go if I have a VIP ticket?

Aldersgate Hall, just inside entrance 4.

What do I get with the VIP ticket?

A personal experience with Jennifer! She will join VIP guests in their private room to share insights into the evening’s talk and take some questions. You will also receive a signed book, a photo op and reserved seating in the Sanctuary.

When does the Sanctuary open?

Doors open at 7:00 p.m., and the event begins at 7:30 p.m. The balcony will be closed.

What will Jennifer be talking about?

We don’t ask to review a speaker’s topic or content, but typically, speakers incorporate any recently published books with a seasonal theme.

Will there be book sales?

Yes! Books will be for sale in the Atrium.

Will Jennifer be signing books?

Yes! Book signing will begin at approximately 9:15 p.m. in the Atrium. You can meet Jennifer, have a brief conversation, and take a photo.

Is there child care?

Limited child care will be available for children 6 months – 12 years old for those who purchase VIP and Dessert and Worship tickets.
