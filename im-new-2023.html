We're Glad You're Here

Welcome!

Welcome to The Woodlands Methodist Church! We’re so glad you found us! Like most families, life is probably busy and a little chaotic these days. Our church is here to help you navigate the many challenges facing individuals and families. You’ll find worship to be uplifting as our pastors share stories about a loving God full of peace and hope. We also have many people who can’t wait to show you around. They want to help your family find a place to connect and develop deeper relationships so life, even a busy one, is full of meaning and purpose.

FIND YOUR SERVICE

At The Woodlands Methodist Church, we value being one church with one mission and multiple expressions of worship. We are comprised of four worship communities that offer a distinctive, unique expression of worship. Traditional, Harvest, Loft, and the Church at Woodforest combine biblical teaching, prayer, worship through music and song, communion and baptism to offer many opportunities to worship in community.
