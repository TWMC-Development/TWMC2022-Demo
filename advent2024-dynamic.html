Christmas is a season filled with joy — from the gatherings to the gifts, from the lights to the music. But the true joy of Christmas is Jesus. Let’s embrace that joy and let it fill our hearts, our homes and our communities.

This Christmas, join us at The Woodlands Methodist Church and celebrate the joy of the season. “Let every heart prepare Him room” as we rejoice in God’s gift to us all — Jesus.

COVENANT EN ESPAÑOL

Redescubra este maravilloso tiempo de Navidad en el que Dios se hizo hombre y quiso vivir entre nosotros y con nosotros. El Servicio de Navidad del Pacto es una oportunidad para conectarnos con Dios mientras cantamos villancicos, leemos las Escrituras y escuchamos un mensaje navideño. Ofrecemos opciones de cuidado infantil para niños menores de 10 años o, si lo prefieres, tus hijos pueden quedarse contigo durante el culto. También hay una sala de llanto justo al lado del santuario.

Después del servicio navideño, disfrute de una obra navideña familiar de 30 minutos.

Outreach Opportunities

Angel Tree

November 16 ‐ December 1

Angel Tree, a ministry of Prison Fellowship, provides a pathway for moms and dads in prison to strengthen the relationship with their children through the giving of Christmas gifts. Help make Christmas special for these families by purchasing toys and clothing for these children. Gift tags for children will be available for pick-up before and after services November 16-December 1. Find more information at twmc.org/outward

Christmas Miracles

November 16 ‐ December 8

Christmas Miracles, a TWMC ministry, provides Christmas gifts and clothing for underserved children and senior adults in Montgomery County. Come by the Atrium on The Woodlands Campus November 13 and 14, 9 a.m.-1 p.m. and select your tags early. Tags will be available for selection before and after services November 16-December 8. Find more information at twmc.org/outward

Counseling

The holidays bring out different emotions in each of us. Sometimes it can be a time when we struggle to feel festive like those around us. We want to walk beside you and assure you of God’s presence as you navigate the holiday season.

Prayer Ministry

If you’re experiencing some difficult days during this holiday season because you’ve lost a loved one, or life is just hard right now, we’re here to support you. We offer pastoral counseling, support groups and prayer teams to help you find the hope of Christ.
