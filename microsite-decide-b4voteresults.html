Vision Night | May 7, 2023

Join us for an inspiring evening of music and message as we envision the future of our ministry together at The Woodlands Methodist Church. Vision Night begins at 6:00 p.m. in the Sanctuary of The Woodlands Campus and will conclude with TWMC member-only voting to join the Global Methodist Church beginning at 7:00 p.m. Nursery care and limited child care are available by registration only for Vision Night (infant – age 4). Visit the link below to register your child.

Child Care Registration
Preregistration is NOT required to cast a ballot.

Anyone can cast a ballot, but only current TWMC member votes will be counted and included in the official vote total. (The unique information you provide on your ballot will be used to verify membership status in our most current database records.)

Voting in Person

In-person member voting will be available following the Vision Night program mentioned above. Additionally, in-person voting will be available in the main administration office of The Woodlands Campus from Monday, May 8 – Thursday, May 11, 2023, during regular business hours, 8:30 a.m. – 4:30 p.m.

Voting Online

Online and mobile app voting opens at the conclusion of Vision Night, beginning at 7:15 p.m., Sunday, May 7, 2023, and closes at 4:30 p.m. on Thursday, May 11, 2023. You must have a TWMC user account to vote using the church website or through the TWMC mobile app. Questions? Email database@twmc.org

Need to set-up a new user account? Visit twmc.org/newaccount

Recover your login information or reset your password here: twmc.org/forgotusername

For support questions, email database@twmc.org

Voting Results

Results of our affiliation vote will be announced in all worship services on Mother’s Day, May 14, 2023. Results will also be emailed to church members and posted on this webpage after worship services conclude on May 14.

Church Membership

Joining the church for the first time or verifying your member status at TWMC will ensure your ballot gets counted in the churchwide vote.

The Woodlands Campus

We receive new members every weekend after each of our on-campus worship services in Robb Chapel, the Sanctuary, Harvest Worship Center and Loft Worship Center. When you exit the worship service, look for a banner that says “Join Here” or go to the Connection Point. We have volunteers who will be there and will walk you through the new member process.

Woodforest Campus

Membership information for Woodforest is available by stopping by their campus office before or after worship each week.

Visit this page on our website to learn more about membership at TWMC.
