Our Schools

Children of The Woodlands

Children of The Woodlands is a non-profit ministry of The Woodlands Methodist Church. Our preschool was the first church-related preschool in the community, founded in 1981. We are dedicated to helping students learn and grow in a loving, stimulating Christian environment. Our purpose is to reflect the highest values while providing the best of care and a quality education for children. Everyday classroom experiences are interpreted in ways consistent with the Bible and Christian values. Students of all faiths are welcome.

Classrooms are filled with hands-on learning experiences, creating a dynamic, Christian learning environment. Low student-to-staff ratios allow us to provide an enriching learning environment that meets the needs of students at his or her developmental level. All preschool classrooms have a teacher and a classroom assistant which allows students many opportunities for individualized and small group learning.

The Woodlands Methodist School

The Woodlands Methodist School is a non-profit ministry of The Woodlands Methodist Church. Children Of The Woodlands was the first church-related preschool in the community, founded in 1981. Eventually kindergarten, Grades 1 and 2 were added, and The Woodlands Methodist School was formed in 2011, dedicated to helping students learn and grow in a loving, stimulating Christian environment. We now offer PreK – 8th grade on our campus located in the heart of The Woodlands, Texas. Our purpose is to reflect the highest values while providing the best of care and a quality education for children. Everyday classroom experiences are interpreted in ways consistent with the Bible and Christian values. Students of all faiths are welcome.

Classrooms are filled with hands-on learning experiences, creating a dynamic, Christian learning environment with high academic success. Low student-to-teacher ratios allow us to provide an enriching learning environment that meets the needs of students at his or her developmental level. Our students are afforded many opportunities for individualized and small group learning. Our developmental program includes reading, writing, social studies, math and science, along with extra enrichment in Bible, art, computers, physical education, and music activities.
