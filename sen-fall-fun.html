Find the perfect activity using activity level ratings

Our activity level ratings provide an understanding of the physical expectations for each of our events. These descriptions summarize the ease of rigors of activities, as well as information regarding length of time standing or walking.

Level 1: Easy

Adults in good health with overall good mobility, and who are comfortable walking on flat surfaces, and can climb stars. Able to sit for long periods of time.

Level 2: Moderate

Standing and walking for short periods. May include slight activity for participation as well as climbing on and off the bus.

Level 3: Moderate/Active

Event is more active and participatory. This event is long periods of walking and climbing on and off the bus. Quite possible this event may include an overnight component to rest and recuperate.
