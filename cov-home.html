At Covenant Church at Creekside, we embrace authenticity, connect in intergenerational community and serve our neighbors while deepening our relationship with God.

Latest Message

The unity of the church is a gift from Jesus, meant to sustain us after his departure. But what does it really mean to be “the church”? What role does the church play in the world and in the lives of its members? We find answers to these questions in the different names the Bible gives to this beloved entity of God’s people on earth. When we gather and bear witness to God’s love, we become an “assembly,” walking together in faith. We function as one body, coming together as a flock, joining the saints who have gone before us, and continuing the legacy as part of the royal priesthood. But our identity goes beyond just being workers in God’s mission. We are his beloved children, brought back to him through the sacrifice of Christ. We are his prized inheritance and his cherished bride. Let’s explore these names and see how they can guide us to become more than just a Sunday gathering. Let’s discover how to live as the true “ekklésia” of God.
