Discipling Isolated Believers

Satellite technology remains a crucial tool in our mission, as it operates beyond the bounds of censorship and monitoring. Partnerships with organizations like SAT-7 are vital to our efforts in nurturing the faith of new Christians residing in closed areas, where openly congregating as a Christian community is often impossible. Leveraging technology, organizations like SAT-7 facilitate theological education, delivering the message of salvation to those who find themselves isolated. SAT-7 helps spread the Good News of Christ and offers ongoing discipleship support through fellowship programs. Thanks to the unwavering support of The Woodlands Methodist Church, SAT-7 has been able to extend its outreach and provide counseling to more individuals than ever before.

Helping People in Crisis

Through our partnerships with Family Promise and Habitat for Humanity, we extend a helping hand to those in need, providing them with a place to call home and helping them see the gift of homeownership as coming straight from the hand of God. Many factors can lead people to become unsheltered, and not everyone has the option to turn to family for support. Families who seek refuge with Family Promise are often in the midst of trauma, uncertain about where they will find shelter. Recognizing this need, Family Promise pursued the opportunity to apply for Habitat for Humanity homes, understanding that security and a stable place to live are vitally important, especially for individuals who have experienced homelessness.
