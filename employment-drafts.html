TWMS Director of Admission and Community Relations

SCOPE

The Director of Admission and Community Relations is an integral member of the school leadership team. This position is responsible for the overall management of the recruitment and admittance of students to The Woodlands Methodist School (TWMS), school external communication and marketing, and developing outreach opportunities for students, families and faculty.

The position will be supported by the Head of School, The Woodlands Methodist School Board (Board) and The Woodlands Methodist Church (TWMC).

ADMISSION DUTIES AND RESPONSIBILITIES

  • Provide leadership to all members of the admission team to maintain high standards for customer service to prospective families, church and school families, faculty and staff; cultivate a spirit of hospitality across the school
  • Develop and recommend admission policy and procedures for review and approval by the Head of School
  • Manage the entire admission process, beginning with recruitment, first inquiry, class placement, assimilation through reenrollment
  • Develop and orchestrate protocols and schedules for school tours, candidate interviews, classroom visits, informal and formal assessments
  • Collaborate with school leaders and to create and maintain an Admission Committee to make timely and prayerful enrollment decisions
  • Communicate with families throughout the admission process, and cultivate relationships over time, shepherding newly admitted students during their first few days at school
  • Develop tools and programs to assist with new student and new family assimilation and collaborate with the school leadership team to facilitate student retention
  • Work with School Business Office to maintain accurate enrollment information and annual statistics, and provide regular enrollment reports to the Head of School and the Board
  • Evaluate enrollment data to monitor the effectiveness of the admission process
  • Develop the budget for admission, marketing and outreach in collaboration with other school leaders and Board members, and manage the program within the budget
  • Develop a three year plan to target, nurture, enroll and keep mission appropriate students
  • Develop and lead a support team for the Admissions office
  • Collaborate with and support the Principal and Director of Preschool in areas of growth and planning
  • Maintain high visibility and involvement on campus by participating when available in daily activities, parent meetings, and special events
  • Participate in local and national admission conferences and events
  • Meet school and church requirements for professional growth
  • Assist with school and church events and activities when needed

COMMUNICATION AND MARKETING

  • Serve as the first point of contact for questions about admission, enrollment, class offerings and advertising
  • With the support of the TWMC Creative Services team, develop marketing and promotional media materials, events, social media and advertising campaigns to capture the attention of mission appropriate families and the community in general
  • Track effectiveness of marketing campaigns post-program
  • Maintain flexible schedule to represent the school at community gatherings, events and conferences
  • Establish relationships with other private and Christian schools and promote effective communication among schools
  • Obtain and analyze annual competitive tuition information and program information from local schools
  • Educate and enlist school faculty to support and assist as needed in the admission process
  • Develop relationships with TWMS staff and families
  • Promote strong relationships with other TWMC ministries and church families
  • Collect information for the Elementary and Middle School monthly newsletters
  • Publish the newsletter for TWMS
  • Communicate with media any website changes for the school
  • Verify and update all information on the website so that it is up-to-date
  • Update school pictures on Facebook and postings
  • Attend weekly Administrative meetings

OUTREACH

  • Oversee network of parents, former parents and alumni to assist in the admission program and to benefit the school
  • Develop and coordinate opportunities for student and parent involvement in age-appropriate outreach activities such as food drives, mission events, Children’s Ministry offerings and worship
  • Provide support to TWMS parent group(s) in fundraising, meetings, and special events
  • Participate in church capital improvement plans in conjunction with school and church leadership teams
  • Work with Preschool leaders to create strong relationships with faculty and families that will contribute to robust Elementary School enrollment
  • Serve as the liaison with pastoral staff; encourage their awareness and involvement

QUALIFICATIONS

  • Bachelor’s Degree in business, marketing, education or a related field
  • Exceptional verbal and communication skills
  • Strong organizational, management and computer skills
  • Highly motivated and able to work collaboratively
  • Ability to problem solve and think creatively
  • Understanding of marketing principals as they relate to student admissions
  • Ability to develop a long-range plan to execute for the benefit of TWMS continued growth
  • Ability to exercise discretion and utmost professionalism in confidential and sensitive matters
  • Five or more years of experience working in a school or other academic setting
  • Thorough knowledge of the Christian education philosophy
  • Committed Christian

APPLICATION:

Submit resume to employment@twmc.org. Additional information can be found on our school website at www.twmschool.org.
