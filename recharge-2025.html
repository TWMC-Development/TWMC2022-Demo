Keynote

Jennifer Maggio
CEO and Founder of The Life of a Single Mom

Topic: TBA

+ Personal Bio +

Jennifer Maggio

Jennifer Maggio is a five-time author, speaker and CEO of the national nonprofit The Life of a Single Mom®. Her personal journey through homelessness, abuse and single parenting has been used to inspire millions. She has been featured in countless media venues, including The New York Times, The 700 Club, Moody Radio, Family Life Radio, Power Women and many others. She has been named one of the Top 10 Most Influential Leaders in America, an Influential Woman in Business, and a She Leads award winner. She loves Jesus with all her heart and has a deep desire to see others living in freedom.

 

Melissa Williams
Comedian, Single Mother

Emcee

+ Personal Bio +

Melissa Williams

While living in Arizona, Melissa Williams' parents got married 13 days before she was born. After their divorce, Melissa moved to Texas with her mom and two siblings. In high school, she broke the record for the longest false start to date (or ever) in addition to failing her floral design class in the same year. She has never been married, but she delivered an 11-pound, 4-ounce baby. (If you’re wondering, they both survived.) Her daughter is now an amazing 27-year-old woman. Melissa is still in awe that she managed to keep another human alive this long. She loves time with her family, who live in the same town as her. (It is important to note that her Thanksgiving turkey has been voted “the best” in her family three years in a row.) Growing up with a stutter, Melissa spent years in speech therapy and never dreamed she would be speaking in front of hundreds of people. By the grace of God, she has used theater and the creative arts to overcome her speech impediment and is now invited to stages throughout the U.S. to make people laugh. Her speech therapist would be so proud — and maybe a little shocked! She will make you laugh. She will make your friends laugh, and she may even make that one person in your life that hasn't smiled in 17 months or even years laugh! She has been a keynote speaker at a wide variety of events. Whether it be a women's retreat, church or corporate function, party or wedding, she has brought clean comedy to captive audiences through sharing relatable stories of everyday life. She brings laughter that serves as medicine. She laughs out loud and thinks everyone else should too. (Like that one time she was asked to leave 59 Diner because her laugh was too loud. Yes. That happened!)
Life is better when laughing together.

BREAKOUT GROUPS

Rev. Karen Tyler
Pastor of Worship and Evangelism
The Woodlands Methodist Church

Topic: Spiritual Pulse

+ Personal Bio +

Rev. Karen Tyler

Karen’s single-parent journey began in 2017, and while she would describe her life testimony as one of God’s relentless faithfulness, it was through her singleness that she saw more clearly God’s heart for her and the brokenness of her situation as well as His faithfulness in new and amazing depths. She is the mom of five children and the “Sasha” to five grandchildren. Her oldest children, Jessica and Michael, journeyed through foster care before coming into Karen’s family by adoption. Her youngest children, Kate, Emma and William, are completing their collegiate journeys at various universities in Texas.

Because of her love for people who are in hard places, in 2009 Karen began a nonprofit that provides personal care items to single moms and their children. Additionally, in 2017 she founded an organization that provides rocket stoves and water filters for women in Guatemala so that women in rural villages can be spared from respiratory diseases and waterborne illnesses that are prevalent in that environment.

Karen resides in The Woodlands, Texas, where she serves as the Pastor of Worship and Evangelism at The Woodlands Methodist Church.

Niki Brogger
Psychotherapy, LCSW
 

Topic: Trauma of Loss

+ Personal Bio +

Niki Brogger

Niki (she, her, hers) is a trauma and relational therapist with experience assisting clients with processing and healing from childhood wounds, spiritual and relational trauma, and chronic illness. Niki is a Licensed Clinical Social Worker (LCSW), earned her master in social work through Louisiana State University, and continues postgraduate training on Family of Origin, recovery from codependency, EMDR (Eye Movement Desensitization and Reprocessing) and Somatic Experiencing. She is an SE Practitioner and has received the SEP certificate. She encourages clients through their journey to healing, serving as a facilitator to help meet predetermined goals. Niki uses her vast mental health expertise to support and cheer on her clients in their healing.

Laura Brimer
Registered Dietitian, Owner of MakeWell Nutrition
 

Topic: Meal Planning on a Budget

+ Personal Bio +

Laura Brimer

Laura Brimer is a Texas native and has lived in and around The Woodlands for 11 years. She and her husband Tom have two kids, Thomas (6) and Bailey (3). Laura has been a registered dietitian for 10 years and has worked with patients ranging from premature babies to the elderly and all diseases and conditions in between. Her passions include her family, being outside, finding a project, and cooking for the ones she loves. Through her experience with patient care, Laura realized how important it is to know how to grocery shop and how to prepare healthy meals on a budget. She also started meal prepping for her friends and family about five years ago and realized quickly how convenient it made life. For these reasons, she wants to share her expertise and empower others to learn how to find quality ingredients to create nourishing meals and feed happy, healthy families.

Julie Crocker
M.A., M.Ed., B.Ed.
Counselor at Magnolia Creek Counseling

Topic: Technology and Kids

+ Personal Bio +

Julie Crocker

Julie Crocker is a Licensed Professional Counselor Associate in the state of Texas. She has worked in education and ministry for 25+ years. As her growing community emerged from Covid, she saw the alarming need for practicing Christian counselors. Julie decided to leave her position in children's ministry to open her counseling practice, Magnolia Creek Counseling, where she serves children, teens and families. In addition to a bachelor's degree in education from Samford University, she also holds a master's degree in college administration from the University of Arkansas and a master's degree in counseling from Liberty University. She is passionate about the topic of fear, worry and anxiety and our kids. She is supervised by Jennifer J Cain, LPC-S, RPT-S.

Tim Blanchard
CFP®, MBA
Wealth Manager

Topic: Finances and Budgeting

+ Personal Bio +

Tim Blanchard

Tim Blanchard brings 24 years of experience in financial planning and advising to Rhame & Gorrell Wealth Management. A CERTIFIED FINANCIAL PLANNER™ professional, Tim also holds Series 7, 66, and 63 licenses. Tim’s journey into financial services began as a client himself, where he witnessed firsthand the transformative power of having a clear financial strategy. This experience inspired his career, starting in 1998 as a financial planner with Ameriprise Financial Services, where he also taught investment courses to future CFP® professionals preparing for their exams. For 13 years, Tim worked with Charles Schwab, where he managed relationships with over 350 client households, providing tailored financial guidance through planning meetings, educational seminars and support during challenging economic times. During his decade-long tenure at the Houston Food Bank, Tim served as a marketing specialist, where he strengthened donor relations within the food industry and supported the organization’s mission to combat hunger. His efforts helped forge critical partnerships that ensured food security for countless families. Tim’s passion for service began earlier in his career as a volunteer coordinator at the Jersey Food Bank, where he managed impactful volunteer projects, including coordinating efforts with notable individuals like Bruce Springsteen. These experiences highlight Tim’s dedication to making a difference both in his community and beyond.
