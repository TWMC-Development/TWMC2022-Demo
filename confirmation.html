Confirmation

CONFIRMATION

Confirmation is a big step in your child’s personal faith journey, readying him or her for a life of discipleship and Christian service. Our Confirmation program prepares your 6th grader to claim the Christian faith as his or her own.

In addition to weekly classes, your student will participate in service projects within the church and our community, as well as have the opportunity to attend both a fall and spring retreat which includes parents.

CONFIRMATION SCHEDULE 2021 - 2022

The Confirmation journey follows the school year calendar beginning in August and ending in May. Classes meet on Sunday mornings at 9:30 a.m. or 11:00 a.m at the Family Life Center on The Woodlands Campus and 12:00 p.m. at Woodforest. Within a small group, your student will develop a spiritual foundation while building valuable relationships with the other students and the adult leaders of their group.

In addition to weekly classes, your student will participate in service projects within the church and our community, as well as have the opportunity to attend both a fall and spring retreat which includes parents.
