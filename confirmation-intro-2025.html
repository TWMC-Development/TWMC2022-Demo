WHAT IS CONFIRMATION?

Confirmation is a sacred rite of passage in which baptized believers publicly affirm their faith in Jesus Christ and commit to following Him as faithful disciples. All incoming 6th graders—whether already baptized or not—are welcome to participate. Those who have not yet been baptized will have the opportunity to receive baptism prior to the Confirmation ceremony. Through a year-long journey of discipleship rooted in Scripture and supported by Christian community, confirmands are spiritually formed to become full members of The Woodlands Methodist Church—empowered by the Holy Spirit and sent to live on mission for the Kingdom of God.
