Are you ready to G.R.O.W.? Join a Start Here class! Start Here classes offer opportunities to explore faith, learn more about the Bible, and take the next step in your discipleship. We offer two types of Start Here Classes for you to experience. Everyone is invited to join a class and start the journey to a life-changing relationship with Jesus.

Start Here: Faith

Start Here: Faith is designed to explore faith, the Bible and Christianity. You’ll enjoy conversations where you can learn, grow and ask questions. If you are just curious about faith, new to the church, returning to church after time away or interested in learning more, this class is for you!

Register now

Start Here: Grow

Start Here: Grow covers developing a lifestyle of discipleship. If you want to be intentional about your walk with Jesus in all aspects of your faith, join this study. Begin transforming your life with real changes when we have in-depth discussions about the G.R.O.W. Points: Gather in Community, Raise Up, Outward Focus and Walk with God. Take this class and you’ll find yourself on the pathway to a deeper faith.

A Pathway to a Deeper Faith

Jesus' call "to follow me" is an invitation to begin a life-long journey to explore and apply God's Word, grow in maturity, love our neighbors, share our faith and, ultimately, become more like Him. G.R.O.W. Points is a pathway to intentionally connect and live out your faith at The Woodlands Methodist Church. No matter where you are in your faith, we are excited to journey with you.
