Safari Stop Indoor Playground

Safari Stop is open to the public for free play. Safari Stop is appropriate for children ages 3 through 9. Please do not leave children unattended.

HOURS

Sunday – Thursday | 12:00 – 6:00 p.m.
Friday and Saturday | 8:00 a.m. – 6:00 p.m.

Location

Safari Stop is located across from the Fireside Cafe at the north end of the main building.
Get direction →

Rules for Play

  • Children must be supervised by an adult at all times.
  • Playscape is designed for children ages 5 - 9.
  • Please remove shoes and place them in the sneaker keeper before using the play equipment.
  • Adults should use caution using play equipment. Do not place children on lap when sliding.
  • Slide one at a time in a forward seated position, with your feet first.
  • No climbing on the outside of the play equipment.
  • No running.
  • No food, gum or drinks.
  • No climbing up the slides.
  • Please do not bring toys or other items into the play equipment
  • Be safe, have fun!

Sunday Service Play Hours
