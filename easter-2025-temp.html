In the story of Easter, the crown of thorns symbolizes the profound sacrifice made for the greater good. It serves as a reminder of the suffering and pain Christ endured.

But the story doesn’t end there. Christ’s sacrifice transforms into an eternal promise of hope through the resurrection. Easter is a testament to the transformative power of sacrificial love.

From the piercing crown of thorns to the radiant resurrection, Easter tells the story of sacrifice, faith and love.

ASH WEDNESDAY

Ash Wednesday is the beginning of Lent, the 40 days before Easter. The service for Ash Wednesday is a time when we begin a season of remembering the sacrifices of Christ on His journey to the cross and preparing our hearts for the journey toward Easter.
